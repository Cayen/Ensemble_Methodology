SCHRÖDINGER STATE PROTOCOL
Certain systems can exist in simultaneous operational states.

Do NOT classify systems as simply:

OPEN

CLOSED

RECOVERED

FAILED

Instead classify across four dimensions:

Dimension	Question
Physical	Can goods physically move?
Commercial	Will markets use the route?
Political	Who controls access?
Financial	Is insurance/financing available?
