FAILURE MODES


Attribution Collapse - when one model makes an attribute hallucination and the others pile on even if it was a figment. 

Narrative Drift - Goes positive or negative depending on the user and not what the hard facts are saying. Even AI have biases 

Probability Inflation - when one gives a number and suddenly ever model has to compound that number without a reason for it to have been given. 

Confirmation Cascade - Everyone confirms it without evidence. 

Date Drift - when a brand new news article presents old news as new. 

Watchlist Accumulation - when your watch list fills up cause you have no way of unloading (positive or negative) the item. 

Mechanism Drift - When there are too many outcomes from one single source despite that source being unrelated to anything that thing is drifting into (Super El nino suddenly driving down the Japanese Yen)

Vector Inflation - When one Vector becomes way more important or way more connected then what it actually is. 

Political Contamination - Partisan news pulling the evidence one way or another to fit the demographic they are catering to

Source Echo Chamber - When 25 papers all name the same source, but no one new is confirming it.
